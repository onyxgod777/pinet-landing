What is Pi Network?
Pi Network is a new digital currency and ecosystem developed by Stanford PhDs. It allows everyday people to mine cryptocurrency directly from their mobile phones without draining battery or using background data. With over 55 million engaged members, Pi is building the world's most inclusive peer-to-peer economy.
Is Pi Network free?
Yes! Pi is completely free to mine. It costs nothing to download the app and start mining. There's no hidden subscription, no energy cost, and no special hardware required.
Does Pi mining drain my battery?
No. Pi mining uses the Stellar Consensus Protocol (SCP), not Proof-of-Work. It does not run computations on your device. The app just does a lightweight weekly check-in. It won't drain your battery or use background data.

Can I buy and sell Pi?
Pi is listed on multiple exchanges. Within the Pi ecosystem, you can spend Pi directly with merchants. Pi's vision is to become a widely-used everyday currency, not just a speculative asset.
